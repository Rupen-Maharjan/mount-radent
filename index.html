Introduction

Mount Radiant English Boarding High School is an educational institutional of exemplary standard, established in 2006 A.D. in Tokha-15, Gongabu, Kathmandu. It has been running by a team of academic professionals. It has been transforming its activities towards knowledge base learning institution. It is established with the aim of social welfare in one hand and to provide quality education on the other hand. The school applies scientific methodology for the upliftment of moral and intellectual character of the students to meet the global challenges in the twenty-first century. Institution feels pride in having great reputation for producing excellent and agile brains in the contest of globalized phenoena.

AIMS

The motto of MREBHS “EDUCATION FOR ALL” speaks the aim of the school; it will set a platform for the students to up hold the motto.

MREBHS aims to empower its students to be compassionate, responsive to our culture and heritage and become an ideal citizen at the country in particular and the world general. It moulds academically excelling, holistic person with a scientific temperament in an environment, sharing and caring.
